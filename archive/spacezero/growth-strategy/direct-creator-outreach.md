# Direct Creator Outreach Strategy

**Date:** 2026-03-07
**Decision:** Skip influencer platforms. Do direct outreach to gaming/sandbox/building creators on TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

**Why:** We evaluated 24 influencer marketing platforms (see `influencer-platform-comparison.md`). None meet our quality bar. The space is immature -- no Stripe/Notion-level product exists. Lurkit and Collabstr are the best fits but both have rough edges. At our stage, direct outreach gives us full control over quality and relationships.

---

## The Three Playbooks

### 1. How to Find Creators
**File:** `manual-creator-discovery-playbook.md`

Covers TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram discovery methods with specific search terms, hashtags (tiered from high-volume to long-tail), free tools (Social Blade, HypeAuditor, Modash, vidIQ), community channels (subreddits, Discord servers), and a daily 30-45 minute discovery workflow.

**Key insight:** Search by content style, not game title. You're looking for creators who make satisfying build timelapses, ASMR construction, and creative sandbox content -- that visual language maps directly to SpaceZero.

### 2. How to Reach Out and What to Offer
**File:** `direct-outreach-playbook.md`

Covers ideal creator profile (5K-50K follower sweet spot), engagement benchmarks by platform, compensation structures (product seeding at volume, hybrid deals for micro, anchor partnerships for mid-tier), DM and email templates, follow-up cadence, FTC compliance, content rights, negotiation tactics, and a three-tab spreadsheet structure for tracking.

**Key numbers:**
- Target engagement: 7%+ TikTok, 4%+ YouTube, 5%+ Instagram (micro tier)
- Budget: $4,500-$11,500 minimum for pre-launch campaign (~30-55 pieces of content)
- Response rates: 5-10% cold DM, 20-35% warm email
- Lead with product seeding (50-100 creators, $0), upgrade best performers to hybrid deals

### 3. Who to Target First
**File:** `creator-targeting-research.md`

Identifies specific creators across 8 categories: Minecraft builders, cozy gaming, Tiny Glade/satisfying builders, mobile gaming reviewers, ASMR gaming, city builders, Roblox builders, and general gaming TikTok. Includes tiered outreach priorities and communities to monitor.

**Priority targets (Tier 1 -- reach out first):**
- @cozysquirrelyt (TikTok) -- already asked audience if they want more building games
- @cozygamingclub (TikTok, ~94.6K) -- discovers new cozy games

## Execution Plan

**Week 1-2: Discovery**
- 30-45 min/day manual search using the discovery playbook
- Target: find 50-100 qualified creators
- Set up tracking spreadsheet (three-tab structure from outreach playbook)
- Set up TikTok Creator Marketplace account

**Week 3-4: Outreach**
- Warm up Tier 1 creators (follow, engage, comment for 3-7 days)
- Send personalized outreach to top 30-50 creators
- Product-seed 50-100 nano creators with TestFlight/early access

**Week 5-6: Activate**
- Negotiate hybrid deals with responsive micro creators ($100-$300 each)
- Lock in 2-5 anchor partnerships ($500-$2,000 each)
- First creator content goes live

**Ongoing:**
- 5-10 new creators discovered per week
- Monitor hashtags: #buildinggames, #cozygaming, #tinyglade, #indiegames
- Amplify creator content on SpaceZero's official channels

## SpaceZero's Outreach Advantages

1. **Build timelapses are naturally viral.** One 20-min session = 5-7 videos. Creators get content, we get exposure.
2. **Mobile-native.** Easy for creators to capture and share. No complex PC setup.
3. **Pre-launch exclusivity.** "Be one of the first to play" is genuine, not manufactured.
4. **Satisfying building niche is proven.** Tiny Glade has 17.7M TikTok posts. The audience exists and is hungry.
5. **Creative freedom is built in.** Every creator's build is unique -- content is differentiated by default.
