## The ZeroCap Creator Pitch

Every creator on this list would receive some version of:

> "Point your phone at any object -- a shoe, a coffee mug, your dog's favorite toy -- and AI turns it into a cute living pet with its own personality. The before/after transformation IS the content. No production needed."

The content creates itself because:
1. The transformation is visually dramatic (real object → cute creature)
2. The output is personal (it came from YOUR object)
3. Every pet has a unique personality from 6M trait combinations (unexpected, funny)
4. The before/after format is the highest-performing content type on TikTok
