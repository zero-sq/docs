# Influencer / UGC / Creator Platform Audit

Date: 2026-02-28

Audit of 17 influencer, UGC, and creator marketing platforms to verify operational status as of February 2026.

---

## 1. Heepsy (heepsy.com)

- **Website:** Live
- **Recent news:** Merged with Moonio to create a combined tool. No acquisition by a third party. Active reviews on G2, Capterra, and GetApp through 2026. Pricing listed at $89-$369/month.
- **Activity signals:** Blog and review sites show 2026-dated content. Database claims 11M+ influencers across Instagram, YouTube, TikTok.
- **Verdict: ACTIVE**

---

## 2. Modash (modash.io)

- **Website:** Live
- **Recent news:** Raised $12M Series A (led by henQ). Published blog content as recently as January 2026. Held a 2026 insights event. Active Shopify app integration. 350M+ creator database.
- **Activity signals:** Regular blog updates, active community events, ongoing product development. TechCrunch coverage in Oct 2024.
- **Verdict: ACTIVE** -- one of the healthier platforms on this list

---

## 3. SARAL (getsaral.com)

- **Website:** Live
- **Recent news:** Founded 2022 (Mumbai). Claims 200+ brands, $2.1M avg incremental revenue per brand, 7.68x ROAS. Some user complaints about buggy Gmail integrations, hidden fees, and sluggish onboarding.
- **Activity signals:** G2 reviews current through 2025. Active pricing page, academy section. Small but functioning.
- **Verdict: ACTIVE** -- operational but showing some quality concerns
