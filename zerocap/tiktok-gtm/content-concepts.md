# Zerocap content concepts

Tracking what trending concepts we've used (or plan to use) for videos, and decisions about content direction.

## Content direction

**Show the real app flow, not a virtual influencer.** The transformation itself is the hook — point phone at real object, pet appears. No polished brand character needed. Virtual influencers work when you already have an audience; we have 0 followers. The object choice is what makes each video different and shareable.

Format: real object → point phone → pet appears. Dead simple. The trending object is the hook that gets people to stop scrolling.
