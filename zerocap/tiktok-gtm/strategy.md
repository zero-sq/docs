# ZeroCap TikTok GTM: 10M Views

**Date:** 2026-02-27
**Goal:** 10 million impressions across short-form video platforms
**Product:** ZeroCap (AI Tamagotchi app)
**Approach:** AI-generated videos + game footage + meme formats — faceless, viral-optimized

---

## The mission

Hit 10M views/impressions through organic short-form video content. No paid ads. AI-generated content and game footage only — no humans on camera. Meme-driven, virality-first.

This is the execution arm of the [growth strategy](../../research/growth-strategy.md) and the [Q1-Q2 growth playbook](../../planning/q1-q2-2026-growth-playbook.md). Those docs have the math. This directory is where we actually do it.

## Account setup

### Email

Use `@0.space` — the main app domain. Something like `tiktok@0.space` or `social@0.space`. Not personal email, not zerosq.com (that's for formal/corporate stuff).

### Account type

**Creator account** — NOT TikTok Business account. Business accounts restrict the music library, which kills meme content that depends on trending sounds. Creator accounts get full music access + analytics.

### Username candidates

Since there's no human face, the account identity IS the content. The name should describe what people will see, not the company.

| Handle | Angle | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| `@zerocap` | Brand direct | Reserve this regardless. Official brand presence. |
| `@aipetchaos` | Meme/absurdist | Higher viral ceiling, plays into AI-generated weirdness |
| `@whatismypet` | Curiosity hook | Describes the transformation reveal format |
| `@0pets` | Short + brand-adjacent | Clean, brandable, hints at the app |
| `@petsofai` | Category name | Discovery-optimized, describes the niche |

**Recommended two-account strategy:**

1. **Meme/reach account** — Pure viral content. AI-generated pet memes, transformation reveals, trending audio. Don't mention ZeroCap in early videos. Let the content build an audience first. Name: something content-descriptive like `@aipetchaos` or `@whatismypet`.

2. **@zerocap** — Official brand account. Polished content, app demos, pinned explainer. This is where the meme account's bio points: "made with @zerocap."

The meme page drives reach. The brand page converts.

### Content type

All AI-generated and game footage. No live filming. Formats:

- **AI-generated meme videos** — Using tools like Kling, Runway, Pika, or Sora to create viral pet/transformation content
- **Game footage clips** — ZeroCap gameplay, pet creation recordings, satisfying compilations
- **Meme edits** — Trending audio + AI visuals + text overlays
- **Before/after reveals** — Object → AI pet transformation (the core viral loop)

## Platform priority

Based on [research findings](research/short-form-video-content-marketing/key-insights.md):

| Platform | Role | Why |
|----------|------|-----|
| **YouTube Shorts** | Primary growth | 85-90% of creators get better organic reach vs TikTok. Content keeps gaining views for months (vs TikTok's 2-3 day peak). |
| **TikTok** | Secondary + search | Still massive discovery. TikTok-as-search-engine is underrated — people search "AI pet app" and "cute AI" there. |
| **Instagram Reels** | Tertiary + shareability | DM shares are the #1 signal. Design content specifically for "send this to a friend." |

Cross-post everything. Produce once, distribute to all three.
