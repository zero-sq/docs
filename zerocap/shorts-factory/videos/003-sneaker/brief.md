# 003 — Sneaker

## Object
A white sneaker (classic low-top, chunky sole, visible laces)

---

## Pet image prompt (Nano Banana)

**Aspect ratio:** 1:1 (square)

**Animal choice:** Rabbit — fast, bouncy, always ready to bolt. Long ears echo the
trailing laces. White body maps naturally to a white sneaker. Built for speed.

**Creature profile:**
```
Name:        Dashhopper
Type:        Speed
Personality: Restless sprinter. Can't stand still, always bouncing on its toes,
             twitching to take off. Competitive but playful.
Pose:        Crouched in a runner's start position, one foot forward, ears swept
             back like it's about to launch.
Signature:   Its ears ARE untied shoelaces — long, white, fabric laces with
             aglets on the tips, flopping back mid-sprint.
```

```
A cute collectible 3D plush rabbit inspired by a white sneaker. Designed to feel like a
real toy you'd find on a shelf — tactile, huggable, premium build quality.

The rabbit is crouched in a runner's start position, one chunky foot forward, ears swept
back like it's about to launch. Intense focus on its face — locked in, ready to go.

Its round body has the smooth white leather texture of a fresh sneaker, with stitching
lines running along its sides like shoe panel seams. Its belly has a mesh fabric patch
like the breathable tongue of a sneaker. Chunky oversized feet with thick rubber soles —
waffle-pattern tread clearly visible on the bottom. Its long ears are untied shoelaces —
white fabric laces with tiny metal aglets on the tips, swept back mid-sprint. A small
embroidered swoosh-like speed line on its haunch.

Big glossy expressive eyes with bright catchlight reflections, narrowed with competitive
determination. Round body, chibi proportions, powerful stubby hind legs. Color palette:
clean white, light gray stitching, pop of bright red on the sole edge. Soft lighting,
clean white background.
```

**Design notes:**
- Rabbit = speed + bouncy, maps perfectly to sneaker energy
- White leather body + stitching seams = shoe panel construction
- Mesh belly = sneaker tongue breathable fabric
- Shoelace ears with aglets = the signature "that's genius" moment
- Chunky waffle-tread feet = unmistakably sneaker
- Runner's crouch = action pose, not static

laces. Smooth reverse transformation, 3 seconds.
