# Object list

50 real-life objects to scan into pets. Criteria:
- Universally recognizable (kids worldwide know it)
- Visually distinctive (strong shape/color = interesting pet design)
- Mildly absurd or unexpected (drives "wait what?" replay behavior)
- Mix of categories to keep the feed fresh

## Object selection notes

- **Avoid:** Anything too niche, culturally specific, or potentially controversial
- **Prioritize:** Objects that look dramatically different as pets (bigger transformation = more "wow")
- **Batch by setting:** Group objects by location to reuse camera-screen backgrounds efficiently
  - Kitchen batch: Coke, banana, pizza, donut, ketchup, cereal, egg, matchstick, sponge
  - Bathroom/laundry batch: Rubber duck, toilet, toothbrush, toilet paper, detergent pod
  - Desk/bedroom batch: Backpack, controller, pencil, headphones, water bottle, light bulb, AirPods, stapler, battery, key, padlock
  - Outdoor/misc batch: Sneaker, soccer ball, skateboard, basketball, ice cream, rubber ball, clothespin, umbrella, coin, whistle
  - Fashion batch: Croc, hoodie, flip flop, sunglasses, rubber glove, sock
