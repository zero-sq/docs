# Zerocap Discord Server Targeting List

**Last updated:** 2026-03-13
**Purpose:** GTM outreach targeting for Zerocap — an iOS AI companion/tamagotchi app where you point your camera at real-world objects and AI transforms them into cute living pets with personality traits.

---

## Priority Tiers

- **Tier 1 (High Priority):** Direct audience overlap — virtual pet lovers, AI companion users, cozy/cute gaming fans. These people already want what Zerocap offers.
- **Tier 2 (Medium Priority):** Adjacent audiences — AI tool enthusiasts, mobile gamers, creative communities. Need a hook but are predisposed to be interested.
- **Tier 3 (Lower Priority):** Amplifier audiences — content creators, marketing communities, indie builders. Useful for distribution and partnerships, less for direct users.

## Rules of Engagement

1. **Never spam.** Join communities genuinely. Participate in existing conversations before sharing Zerocap.
2. **Read the rules.** Every server has self-promo rules. Violating them gets you banned and hurts the brand.
3. **Lead with value.** Share interesting Zerocap creations, not download links. Let people ask.
4. **Be authentic.** "I'm building a virtual pet app" > "Check out our revolutionary AI platform."
5. **Respect the culture.** Each community has its own vibe. Adapt messaging accordingly.
6. **Track everything.** Note which communities convert, which messaging works, and double down.
7. **Build relationships.** DM moderators before posting promotional content. Ask permission.
8. **One account, consistent identity.** Don't create throwaway accounts. Build a recognizable presence.
