- 25% administrative fee deducted from any refund

### 3. Quality of Content

- Videos start at $59, produced by vetted creators
- Quality is suitable for paid ads (Meta, TikTok) for most ecommerce products
- Multiple reviewers note quality is **adequate for low-to-mid-range products** but not luxury/premium
- Creator compensation is $25-45/video -- this directly impacts quality ceiling
- Average Billo creator earns ~$500/month
- 2 rounds of edit requests included per video
- Billo QA reviews video within 1 business day; creator must fix issues within 2 business days
- Auto-approval after 3 business days if brand doesn't respond
- IP rights: When a creator submits content, they assign IP rights to the brand (important for ad iteration)

**Verdict on quality:** Good enough for performance marketing on Meta/TikTok for consumer products. Not suitable for premium/luxury positioning. Low creator pay means you're getting entry-level talent in many cases.

Sources: [Billo How It Works](https://billo.app/how-billo-works/), [Billo Help Center - Track Video Status](https://help.billo.app/en/articles/6740123-track-the-status-of-your-video), [Influencer Marketing Hub](https://influencermarketinghub.com/billo/)

## Platform 2: Collabstr (collabstr.com)

### 1. Free Tier Limitations

**What you can ACTUALLY do for free (Basic plan):**
- Browse the creator marketplace
- View influencer profiles and pricing
- Purchase collaborations (but pay 10% marketplace fee on every transaction)

**What's LOCKED behind paid plans:**

| Feature | Basic (Free) | Pro ($299/mo) | Premium ($399/mo) |
|---------|-------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Marketplace fee | 10% | 10% | 5% |
| Campaign posting | No | 1 campaign/mo | Unlimited |
| Chat & negotiate | No | Yes | Yes |
| Live analytics tracking | No | 5 posts at a time | More |
| Advanced filters (age, ethnicity, language) | No | Yes | Yes |
| Influencer audience reports | No | 20/month | More |
| Priority support | No | No | Yes |

**Key insight:** The free tier is basically a browse-only storefront. You can see creators and their pricing, but to actually communicate, run campaigns, or access analytics, you need Pro ($299/mo) or Premium ($399/mo). You CAN still buy individual collaborations on the free tier, but at a 10% markup.

Sources: [Collabstr Pricing](https://collabstr.com/pricing), [SpotsaaS Pricing](https://www.spotsaas.com/product/collabstr/pricing), [Ainfluencer's Collabstr Review](https://ainfluencer.com/collabstr/)

### 2. Transaction Fee Structure

**Both sides pay:**
- **Brands:** 10% marketplace fee on Basic, 5% on Premium ($399/mo)
- **Creators:** 15% transaction fee on completed orders (regardless of brand's plan)

This means on a $200 collaboration:
- Brand pays $220 (on Basic) or $210 (on Premium)
- Creator receives $170 (after 15% cut)
- Collabstr takes $50 total (on Basic) or $40 (on Premium)

**Important:** These fees are in ADDITION to the creator's listed price, not built into it.

Sources: [Collabstr Creator Page](https://collabstr.com/creator), [SpotsaaS](https://www.spotsaas.com/product/collabstr/pricing), [Influee Alternatives](https://influee.co/blog/collabstr-alternatives)

### 3. Creator Quality

- **550,000+ creators** in the marketplace (grown from 170K to 550K)
- Self-serve marketplace model means quality varies significantly
- Built-in **fake follower checker** for Instagram and TikTok
- **Influencer Price Calculator** to estimate fair rates
- Creators set their own rates -- transparent pricing
- Covers Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter, Twitch, Amazon
- Search filters: niche/keyword, platform, location (country/city/region), followers, gender, age, price, content type
- Advanced filters (age, ethnicity, language) locked behind Pro plan

**Quality distribution concern:** With 550K+ creators, there's inevitably a wide quality range. The platform doesn't appear to heavily vet creators the way Billo or Clip does. The fake follower checker helps, but brands still need to do due diligence.

Sources: [Collabstr Search](https://collabstr.com/search-influencers), [Collabstr Help Center](https://collabstr.crisp.help/en/article/how-to-search-for-influencers-1ahxs4u/), [Collabstr Expansion PR](https://www.newswire.com/news/leading-influencer-marketplace-collabstr-expands-to-30-countries-21451908)

### 4. Real User Reviews

### 6. Content Licensing

Collabstr does **not** enforce a standard licensing agreement. Instead:
- Usage rights are negotiated between brand and creator
- Collabstr provides guidance and templates for UGC contracts
- Perpetual (in-perpetuity) usage rights typically cost 100-150% of base rate
- Brands must specify: channels, duration, editing rights, ownership in their contracts
- This is a DIY approach -- the platform facilitates but doesn't standardize

**Compared to Billo:** Billo automatically assigns IP rights to the brand on all content. Collabstr leaves this to negotiation, which can be both a pro (flexibility) and a con (complexity).

Sources: [Collabstr Usage Rights Guide](https://collabstr.com/blog/content-usage-rights), [Collabstr UGC Contract Template](https://collabstr.com/blog/ugc-contract)

## Platform 3: Ainfluencer (ainfluencer.com)

### 1. How Is It Actually Free?

**Business model:** Ainfluencer takes a **20% commission from every successful payment on the influencer/creator side.** Brands pay $0 platform fees.

**Verification:** This is confirmed across multiple independent sources (DMPro, Trustpilot reviews, Shopify App Store, Ainfluencer's own documentation). The 20% commission is the primary revenue model.

**Hidden costs for brands:**
- No platform fees, no subscription fees, no transaction fees on the brand side
- However, wallet deposits are **non-refundable** (only credited back to wallet balance, never to original payment method)
- Once you fund the wallet, that money stays on Ainfluencer

**How this affects creators:**
- A creator charging $500 receives $400 after Ainfluencer's cut
- This may mean creators inflate their prices to compensate, or lower-quality creators dominate (because top creators go to platforms with lower commissions)

**Compared to alternatives:**
- Collabstr: 15% creator fee + 10% brand fee = 25% total platform take
- Ainfluencer: 20% creator fee + 0% brand fee = 20% total platform take
- Billo: Platform takes its margin through pack pricing (brand pays $59+/video, creator receives $25-45)

Sources: [DMPro Review](https://dmpro.app/ainfluencer-review/), [AiSchedul Review](https://aischedul.com/ainfluencer-review/), [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/ainfluencer)

### 2. Platform Quality

**The honest assessment:** Ainfluencer is a legitimate platform, not a scam. But it operates at the lower end of the quality spectrum compared to Billo or Insense.

**Evidence of legitimacy:**
- 4.2/5 on Trustpilot (72 reviews)
- 5.0/5 on G2 (5 reviews -- too few to be reliable)
- Active Shopify integration with real reviews
- Claims 70,000 active businesses on platform
- Escrow payment system (funds held until delivery confirmed)
- Supports 6 languages

**Evidence of quality concerns:**
- "Fake influencers" reported on Trustpilot
- Customer service refund refusals documented
- Shopify reviews detail wallet charging errors ($50 charged to card instead of withdrawn from wallet)
- CEO ignored escalation emails in at least one documented case
- Influencers can delete posts shortly after approval (lax commitment tracking)
- Messaging feature reported as non-functional by some users
- Analytics and automation features are "limited"
- No advanced sales tracking or deep integrations

**Verdict:** Functional but rough around the edges. Best for brands with zero budget who are willing to invest time in vetting creators themselves. Not a premium experience.

Sources: [Trustpilot](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/ainfluencer.com), [Shopify Reviews](https://apps.shopify.com/ainfluencer/reviews), [DMPro Review](https://dmpro.app/ainfluencer-review/)

### 3. Creator Database

- **Claimed:** 5M+ influencers (some sources say 2M+, others say 1M active)
- **Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- **Growth:** ~10,000 new creators joining monthly
- **Geographic spread:** Global, searchable by country and city
- **Follower range:** 1K to 10M+
- **Filters:** 20+ including niche, location, follower count, engagement rate, gender, language, audience demographics, hashtags, influencer size (nano/micro/macro/mega)

**Verification concern:** The "5M+" claim is likely inflated. Different sources within Ainfluencer's own materials cite different numbers (500K, 1M, 2M, 5M). The 5M figure may include scraped/indexed profiles rather than actively opted-in creators. One source specifies "1 million active influencers" which is more plausible.

**Quality control:** Unlike Billo (vetted before joining) or Clip (7% approval rate), Ainfluencer appears to have a lower barrier to entry for creators. This means more quantity but less guaranteed quality.

Sources: [Ainfluencer Find Influencers](https://influencers.ainfluencer.com), [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/ainfluencer), [Influencer Marketing Hub](https://influencermarketing.ainfluencer.com/influencer-marketplace/)

### 5. Limitations

Despite marketing claims of "no limitations on any features," real limitations include:

- **Wallet deposits are non-refundable** -- funds from canceled offers go back to wallet, never to your card
- **Limited analytics and automation** -- no deep campaign analytics
- **No advanced sales tracking** -- can't measure true ROI through the platform
- **No deep integrations** -- beyond basic Shopify, limited tech stack connections
- **Lax commitment enforcement** -- influencers can delete posts shortly after approval
- **Support quality inconsistent** -- documented cases of 25+ day response times
- **Instagram and TikTok only** (YouTube being added) -- no Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.
- **No managed service option** -- entirely self-serve, brand does all the work

| **Geographic presence** | Global | 30+ countries |
| **Maturity** | Earlier stage, rougher product | More polished, better UX |

**Bottom line:** Ainfluencer is cheaper but rougher. Collabstr costs more but provides a better user experience and more tools. For truly zero budget, Ainfluencer wins. For slightly more budget and better experience, Collabstr's free tier + 10% fee is the better choice.

### Scenario: First 5-10 UGC Videos for Paid Ads

### Recommendation for SpaceZero
